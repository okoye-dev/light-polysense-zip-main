PolySense HUB

This is the only device that needs an internet connection.
Cellular connection is possible if Wi-Fi or Ethernet are not. The PolySense HUB wirelessly receives sensor data from up to 500 meters away using the LoRaWAN protocol. This means no Wi-Fi, ZigBee, or Bluetooth extenders, and significantly more security for your critical IT infrastructure not to mention less troLbleshooting and ease of setup.

PolySense NODE

The NODE is our multipurpose sensor. It is battery powered and communicates wirelessly over long range radio (LoRaWAN).

It is capable of sensing:

- Ambient Temperature
- Ambient Humidity
- Vibration, acceleration, sound motion & light
- Analog or digital signals from other equipment

PolySense AMP
(20-600 Amps)

No running wires or conduit, installs in minutes.
For assets that are 3-phase or hardwired, we use the AMP. The AMP is a battery-powered sensor that non-invasively measures the electrical activity of the connected asset.
It simply clips over one of of the main wires of the asset and begins collecting eectrical data.

PolySense ICE

The ICE is the ultimate cold storage monitor. It will send you critical alerts when temperatures rise or the door is open for too long. It also records when the door/lid of a freezer or refrigerator is opened to better understand how often the asset is accessed, and how long the door is open on average. Remote Temperature monitoring of -200°C to +1300°C possible with accurate thermocouple.

PolySense BIO

The BIO is your essential monitor for CO2 incubators. It wirelessly measures CO2 levels from 0-10%, along with temperature and humidity. Equipped with LoRaWAN technology, it ensures reliable and real-time communication for optimal monitoring and control of incubator conditions without complicated installation process.

PolySense LEAK

This advanced leak detection sensor harnesses rope-style technology for superior sensitivity and accuracy. The LEAK ensures early identification of liquid leaks to prevent potential damage. Standard rope length is 3 meters. Up to 3 ropes can be connected in series to obtain 9 meters of leak detection.

PolySense BOLT

The BOLT is a smart-plug that communicates wirelessly over LoRaWAN.

What it can do:

- Record how much electricity an asset uses2 and when it uses
- Record how often assets are used and track downtime
- Send alerts when an asset stops/starts using power

PolySense FLOW

The PolySense FLOW is an ultrasonic non-invasive flow meter. Simply clamp onto the pipe, no cutting required. The FLOW fits pipe sizes from 1/4 inch to 2 inch diameters, and is accurate to +/- 2% of the flow rate up to 100 L/min

PolySense DISTANCE

The PolySense DISTANCE is an advanced laser distance sensor designed for high-precision monitoring of iquid and powder levels. Engineered for reliability and easy integration, this versatile sensor is ideal for various industries including manufacturing, food production, and bio-research. Transforms distance data into actionable insights, empowering you to manage resources proactively and efficiently.

PolySense ALERT

The PolySense ALERT is a tri-colored light tower designed to complement text and email notifications by providing immediate, visual status indicators. Tailor the light status to fit your specific application for a fully integrated, multi-channel alert system

PolySense PULSE

The PULSE is our unique additive manufacturing monitoring system.

What The PULSE can do:

- Detect when a build starts or stops
- Predict build failures before they occur
- Alert users via SMS when there are any failures or predicted failure
- Alert users when power is lost to the facility
- Allow users to view a live HD video feed of the build chamber.
