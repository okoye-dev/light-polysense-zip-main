Interactive 3D Facility Mapping

Navigate through a highly interactive 3D virtual tour that serves as a dynamic map of your facility, giving you a visual representation of all your assets, rooms, and workspaces.

Key Features

Real-Time Asset Monitoring

Click on any asset within the 3D tour to instantly view its current status, performance metrics, and utilization data.

On-Demand Directions

Simplify asset accessibility by providing instant directions to any selected machinery or workspace within the facility.

Precision Planning

Utilize the measuring tools within the 3D tour to assess distances and sizes, aiding in precise planning for installations, relocations, or expansions

Virtual Blueprint Creation

Generate accurate floor plans directly from the 3D model, which can be invaluable for renovations, asset relocations, or future expansions

Multi-Location Support

For businesses with multiple facilities, switch between 3D models of different buildings or floors, centralizing your control and monitoring efforts

Off-Site Control

With remote access capabilities, you can tour your facility and monitor assets in real-time from anywhere in the world, ensuring uninterrupted operational control.
