About
PolySense Solutions

Founded in Winnipeg, Canada, PolySense Solutions equips businesses across North America with
advanced, specialized sensors and software for smarter asset and facility management.

Our Mission

Our mission at PolySense Solutions is to simplify and enhance the management of critical environments, such as laboratories and advanced manufacturing facilities. Our cutting-edge monitoring systems install in minutes with no wiring required, providing you with real-time status, health, and usage data of your critical assets.

Backed by several pending Canadian and US patents, our technology offers real-time tracking of energy use, asset utilization, maintenance schedules, and environmental conditions, ensuring you have complete visibility and control over your operations. Imagine transforming the complexity of managing expensive equipment, intricate processes, and critical environments into clear, actionable insights. Our secure cloud-based software unlocks comprehensive visibility over your assets and facilities, enabling proactive management, enhanced productivity, and significant cost savings.
